# Research Plan: Data Privacy and Intellectual Property (IP)

## Overview
Biomedical research involves sensitive patient data and valuable IP. This plan investigates the legal and privacy barriers to using LLMs in these workflows.

## Key Research Questions
1. How do HIPAA and GDPR impact the use of cloud-based LLMs for hypothesis generation?
2. Who "owns" a discovery made by an LLM?
3. What are the technical solutions for privacy-preserving discovery (e.g., on-premise models, federated learning)?

## Methodology
- **Legal Review:** Analyze current patent law and data protection regulations relevant to AI.
- **Technical Audit:** Review privacy-preserving techniques like differential privacy in LLM training.
- **Policy Recommendations:** Synthesize advice for institutional review boards (IRBs).

## Expected Outcomes
- A framework for navigating IP and privacy in AI-driven research.
- Comparison of local vs. cloud deployment risks.
