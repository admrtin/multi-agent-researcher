# Research Plan: Integration of Knowledge Graphs with LLMs

## Overview
LLMs often struggle with factual consistency. This research explores how Knowledge Graphs (KGs) can be integrated with LLMs to provide a structured foundation for biomedical hypothesis generation.

## Key Research Questions
1. How can structured knowledge (e.g., UMLS, Gene Ontology) constrain LLM outputs to biological reality?
2. What are the leading methods for KG-LLM integration (e.g., graph embeddings, prompt-based grounding)?
3. Does KG integration significantly reduce hallucinations in biomedical contexts?

## Methodology
- **Technical Review:** Examine frameworks like "KnowGL" or "Bio-KGR".
- **Benefit Assessment:** Analyze case studies where KG integration corrected LLM errors.
- **Architecture Mapping:** Map different types of biological KGs to specific hypothesis generation tasks.

## Expected Outcomes
- A blueprint for "Neuro-symbolic" hypothesis generation.
- Evaluation of the synergy between structured data and probabilistic language models.
