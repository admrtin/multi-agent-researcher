# Research Plan: Future Directions: Towards Autonomous Science

## Overview
The ultimate goal is the "Self-Driving Lab." This research looks at the future integration of LLM hypothesis generation with robotic experimental platforms.

## Key Research Questions
1. How can LLMs be interfaced with Lab Automation Systems (LAS)?
2. What is the feedback loop between automated hypothesis, robot experiment, and model refinement?
3. What are the long-term implications for the role of the scientist?

## Methodology
- **Technological Forecasting:** Review emerging "Cloud Labs" (e.g., Emerald Cloud Lab, Strateos).
- **System Architecture:** Design a theoretical end-to-end autonomous discovery loop.
- **Expert Interviews:** Summarize visions from leaders in AI and robotics.

## Expected Outcomes
- A vision document for the next decade of automated biomedical discovery.
- Identification of the technical hurdles remaining for full autonomy.
