# Research Plan: Applications in Drug Discovery

## Overview
The pharmaceutical industry is a primary adopter of AI. This research focuses specifically on how LLM-driven hypotheses accelerate target identification and drug repurposing.

## Key Research Questions
1. How are LLMs used to identify novel drug targets from existing literature?
2. Can LLMs effectively hypothesize new indications for existing drugs (repurposing)?
3. What are the success stories and failures of AI-driven drug discovery hypotheses?

## Methodology
- **Industry Case Studies:** Review papers and press releases from companies like Insilico Medicine or Exscientia.
- **Process Mapping:** Analyze the pipeline from LLM hypothesis to molecular docking and synthesis.
- **Economic Impact:** Assess the time and cost savings reported in AI-augmented discovery.

## Expected Outcomes
- A detailed report on LLM impact in pharmacology.
- Identification of specific drug classes most amenable to AI hypothesis generation.
