# Research Plan: Applications in Genomics and Rare Diseases

## Overview
Rare diseases often suffer from a lack of research. This plan explores how LLMs can bridge this gap by generating hypotheses for understudied genetic variants.

## Key Research Questions
1. How can LLMs synthesize disparate data to explain Variants of Unknown Significance (VUS)?
2. Can LLMs generate mechanistic hypotheses for rare phenotypic expressions?
3. What role does LLM-based literature synthesis play in "Diagnostic Odysseys"?

## Methodology
- **Genomic Literature Review:** Study the use of LLMs in interpreting whole-exome and whole-genome sequencing.
- **Tool Evaluation:** Assess tools like GeneGPT or similar LLM-agent wrappers for genomics.
- **Success Metrics:** Evaluate the "diagnostic yield" improvement attributed to AI tools.

## Expected Outcomes
- Evaluation of LLMs as tools for precision medicine and rare disease research.
- A roadmap for integrating LLMs into clinical genomics pipelines.
