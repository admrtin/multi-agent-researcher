# Research Plan: Ethical and Safety Considerations

## Overview
Automated hypothesis generation in medicine carries risks. This research investigates the ethical implications, biases, and safety concerns associated with LLM-driven discovery.

## Key Research Questions
1. What are the potential risks of "hallucinated" hypotheses in clinical research?
2. How do biases in training data affect the fairness and diversity of generated hypotheses?
3. What safety protocols are necessary before an AI-generated hypothesis moves to lab validation?

## Methodology
- **Risk Assessment:** Analyze documented instances of LLM hallucinations in medical literature.
- **Bias Analysis:** Review studies on demographic or geographic bias in biomedical datasets.
- **Ethics Framework:** Synthesize ethical guidelines for AI in scientific discovery.

## Expected Outcomes
- A risk mitigation strategy for AI-driven biomedical research.
- Guidelines for responsible AI use in hypothesis formulation.
